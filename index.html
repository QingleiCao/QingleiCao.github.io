About Me

I'm an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at the Saint Louis University (SLU), where I direct the Computational Intelligence Lab. Before joining SLU, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow/Scholar with Dr. George Bosilca at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville (2023) and a Member of Technical Staff (MTS) at Cerebras Systems (an AI chip startup company), Sunnyville, CA (2022-2023) I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science with Dr. Jack Dongarra in 2022 from the University of Tennessee, Knoxville, USA, and obtained my B.E. and M.E. degrees from Hunan Unversity (2009) and Ocean University of China (2016) respectively. In addition, I served as HPC Software Developer & Research Scientist at the National University of Defense Technology (NUDT), contributing to the development and research of the TH-1A and TH-2 supercomputers (2009-2013). My research interest lies in areas of High-performance Computing (HPC) and Artificial Intelligence (AI) to tackle real-world challenges. Besides research, I love playing sports, including basketball, tennis, and fishing, and reading fantasy novels.

We are always looking for motivated undergraduate students, graduate students (Masters and PhDs) and postdoctoral fellows to join the Computational Intelligence Lab. Don't hesitate to get in touch with Qinglei for more information.
