The Omni Computing Lab started in August 2023 as part of the Department of Computer Science at the Saint Louis University (SLU). Our research spans the areas of High-performance Computing (HPC) and Artificial Intelligence (AI), focusing on

• Parallel and distributed computing;
• task-based runtime system;
• linear algebra algorithms at scale;
• large-scale machine learning & deep learning;
• HPC + AI -> applications, e.g., geospatial modeling like climate and weather prediction.

We are always looking for motivated undergraduate students and graduate students (Masters and PhDs) to join the Omni Computing Lab. Don't hesitate to get in touch with Qinglei Cao for more information.
