The High-performance AI Lab started in August 2023 as part of the Department of Computer Science at the Saint Louis University (SLU). Our research spans the areas of High-performance Computing (HPC) and Artificial Intelligence (AI), focusing on

• task-based runtime system;
• linear algebra algorithms at scale;
• large-scale machine learning & deep learning;

We are always looking for motivated undergraduate students, graduate students (Masters and PhDs) and postdoctoral fellows to join the High-performance AI Lab. Don't hesitate to get in touch with Qinglei Cao for more information.
